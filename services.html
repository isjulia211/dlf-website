air transport

Air transport is as the name suggest, goods are transported via air for urgent items that are needed. DLF has the needy process in place for our clients to get their goods in the fastest time possible. Time is of the essence for air freight and we will always aim to get the fastest possible transit time while keeping in mind the charges for clients. A rough break down of air freight is as below.
Supplier sending goods to origin airport -> goods arriving in Australia -> airport unpack goods -> pick up from warehouse -> delivering it to client

ocean transport

ocean transport

Ocean transport is the bread and butter of any freight industry, due to this, DLF has ensured all our staff is well versed in regards so that we can handle every aspects. Ocean transport can be broken down into FCL and LCL (full container load and less container load), as a rule of thumb, any shipment that is less than 10cubic metre will be advised for LCL service.
DLF will always give the best advise for our clients with regards to the best cost saving solution depending on the situation.
